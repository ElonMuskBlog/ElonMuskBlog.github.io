Why Did I Do It

Elon Musk, March 8, 2008

Why do buy/make the company's that I do? The short answer, I am trying to make the future the one we dream of. Long answer, I don't want to sit on an island all my life, I would have to be on serious drugs for that. Instead I like helping the world accomplish the future we dream of. I want to be part of it and not just watch it happen.
